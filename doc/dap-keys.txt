*dap-keys.txt* Keymaps for debugging

Mappings:
	<F5>                                  *dap-F5*
		Continue
		
	<F7>                                  *dap-F7*
		See last session output

	<F1>                                  *dap-F1*
		Step Into

	<F2>                                  *dap-F2*
		Step Over

	<F3>                                  *dap-F3*
		Step Out

	*<leader>*b                             *leader-b*
		Toggle breakpoint

	*<leader>*B                             *leader-B*
		Toggle conditional breakpoint
